About the Achievement Club

Our approach is unique because it promotes family accountability and encourages a cycle of giving back with a two-generation approach.

The Community Helps You

Families in crisis are connected to a case management service. The primary goal is stabilizing connection to essential services.


You Help Yourself

Families work on their dreams by reaching goals. They build self efficacy and resiliency.


You Help the Community

In this step families give back and engage with their neighbors. This builds local leadership and collective efficacy.

Peer Support

Participants join a group of neighbors to set goals for improvement.

Conditional Cash Transfers

Participants get a cash reward each step along the way for meeting goals.

As an Achievement Club family member, you have the opportunity to set and achieve goals in 5 focus areas within the atmosphere of a supportive group of neighbors
